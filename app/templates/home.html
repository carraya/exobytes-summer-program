Welcome!

The VEX Robotics team 80708X Exobytes based in Virginia was established in 2018. The team is composed of rising seniors, some of which have participated in VEX Robotics since 6th grade. Every year the team has qualified for the World Championship. In addition, they have won numerous national and international awards over the past three seasons. Every year, the students on the team strive for continuous improvement and always give their all to achieve their goals.

About the Team

Exobytes Summer Program

This year, team Exobytes has decided that this summer they will be spreading their knowledge on programming and building to others that wish to learn. They will provide 3 course levels regarding topics ranging from subsystems to advanced motion algorithms.
